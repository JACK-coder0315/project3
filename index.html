Key Insights

1. Carbohydrates drive most of the glucose increase.

2. Protein, fat, and fiber blunt the carb-induced spike.

3. Meal timing influences sensitivity (e.g., breakfast vs. dinner).

4. Individual variation highlights personal metabolic differences.

5. Data robustness is ensured by event count under filters.
