Key Insights

1. Carbohydrates drive most of the glucose rise.

2. Protein, fat, and fiber blunt the spike.

3. Meal timing matters (e.g., breakfast vs. dinner).

4. Individuals vary in response.

5. Event count ensures sample robustness.
