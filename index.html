Dynamic Analysis of Pre-operative Hemoglobin vs. Monitoring Tracks

Background & Research Questions

This visualization explores the relationship between pre-operative hemoglobin levels (hb_pre) and the number of monitoring tracks (n_tracks) across different hospital departments, while also assessing the potential influence of surgery duration (duration_min). Use the play button or slider to switch department views and observe clustering patterns.

Legend

  • X-Axis: Number of Monitoring Tracks (n_tracks)
  • Y-Axis: Pre-operative Hemoglobin (g/dL) (hb_pre)
